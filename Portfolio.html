The last video editing project I worked on was a promotional video for a local business. The goal was to create a visually appealing and engaging video that showcased the business's products and services while highlighting its unique selling points. I started by reviewing the raw footage provided by the client and selecting the best clips to use in the final video. Then, I edited the footage using professional video editing software, adding transitions, music, and sound effects to enhance the overall viewing experience. To keep the video engaging, I included text overlays and animations to highlight key points about the business and its offerings. I also color corrected and graded the footage to ensure a consistent and polished look throughout the video.

The graphic design project I worked on was a branding and marketing campaign for a new product launch. The goal was to create a unique and visually appealing brand identity that would effectively promote the product and attract potential customers. To achieve this, I started by researching the target audience and competition to determine the most effective visual style and messaging. Then, I created a logo and color scheme that aligned with the client's vision and brand values, while also being memorable and easily recognizable. Once the brand identity was established, I created a range of marketing materials, including print and digital advertisements, product packaging, and social media graphics. These materials utilized the brand's visual elements and messaging to create a consistent and cohesive campaign that would resonate with the target audience.

The web development project I worked on was a complete redesign and redevelopment of a website for a small business. The goal was to create a modern and visually appealing website that would effectively showcase the business's products and services while providing a smooth user experience. To achieve this, I started by gathering requirements from the client and creating a detailed plan for the new website, including wireframes and a sitemap. Then, I designed the website using industry-standard design software, creating custom graphics and selecting fonts and colors that aligned with the client's branding. Once the design was approved, I began the development phase, using front-end languages like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create a responsive and dynamic website. I also integrated various third-party tools and services, such as a content management system (CMS), social media widgets, and a contact form.
